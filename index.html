About CMMAI 2026

From mathematical modeling and high-performance computation to AI-driven prediction, this conference delves into how modern tools in applied mathematics can address real-world challenges. With a special focus on environmental and industrial applications, this event brings together brilliant minds to shape future-ready solutions through interdisciplinary research.

The conference aims to bring together researchers, academicians, and industrial experts to explore recent developments at the intersection of computational mathematics, applied modeling, and artificial intelligence. It seeks to provide a platform for presenting innovative algorithms, intelligent systems, and mathematical frameworks that address complex, real-world challenges across disciplines. The conference also intends to foster interdisciplinary dialogue, encourage collaborative research, and promote the application of mathematical methods in emerging areas of science and industry. The event will also emphasize applications in environmental science such as modeling climate systems, predicting pollutant behavior, and simulating ecosystem dynamics. Additionally, it aims to support early-career researchers by providing exposure to contemporary tools, techniques, and trends in computational and applied mathematics.

About College

Bharata Mata College (Autonomous), established in 1965 by His Eminence the late Joseph Cardinal Parecattil, is a nationally accredited first-grade arts and science institution in the aided sector, affiliated to Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam. Managed by the Archdiocese of Ernakulam–Angamaly under the Bharata Mata Educational Trust, the college earned an A+ grade from NAAC in 2019 and ranks in the 100–150 band in NIRF. Guided by its motto “For God and Country”, the college upholds the ideals of love, service, and dedication to the nation, while fostering holistic education, responsible citizenship, and inclusivity across gender, caste, and socio-economic backgrounds.

About Department

The Post Graduate and Research Department of Mathematics at Bharata Mata College, Thrikkakara, established in 1965, is distinguished for its academic excellence and vibrant research culture. Offering undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programmes, the department has produced ten Ph.D. graduates and over 100 research publications in reputed journals. It regularly organises national and international conferences, seminars, and workshops, creating a dynamic platform for scholarly exchange and collaboration. Research thrust areas include Fluid Dynamics, Numerical Analysis, Applied Mathematics, Mathematical Modeling, Graph Theory, Topology, Fuzzy Algebra and Fuzzy Topology.
